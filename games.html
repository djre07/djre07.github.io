This was a game I made with the help of a practice template. It is a topdown shooter game where you play as Fred. You shoot enemies to gain score. The more score you get, the harder is gets. It can infinitely go on until the death of the Fred, who has three lives. I created the pixel art and life mechanic for the game.

This was a practice game I created just for fun. There are two basic levels: one, you collect coins and avoid slimes until the end where you pick up a crown to go to the next level.
